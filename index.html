How Does Wild Cabs Work?

At Wild Cabs, we bring you fast, safe, and affordable cab services right at your fingertips. Whether you’re heading to work, exploring the city, or catching a flight, we’re here to make every ride smooth and stress-free. With a fleet of reliable vehicles and professional drivers, Wild Cabs is your go-to travel companion – anytime, anywhere. Book your ride now and experience the wild side of comfort and convenience!

Request A Cab

We can get you there. Simply request a cab on the app and we'll connect you with a driver immediately.

Pick A Price

Pick the valuation you're willing to pay and your ride is set up. The only surge is the acceleration you get when taking off.

Ride Off To Success!

After matching with your driver and agreeing to its terms, you’ll be all set. Your cab will arrive shortly to pick you up.

Our Story

It all started with a simple idea: making getting around Ludhiana easier and more convenient for everyone. Tired of the usual hassles, we envisioned a service that was reliable, affordable, and just a tap away. And so, Wild Cabs was born, driven by the desire to provide a smoother ride for our community.
